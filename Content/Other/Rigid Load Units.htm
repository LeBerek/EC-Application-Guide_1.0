Rigid Load Units

In exceptional transports, goods are usually considered once loaded on individual wagons. Goods longer than the maximum loading length of the wagon, and beyond the wagon length, can also be transported by rail when certain restrictions are observed. Restrictions for long goods exceeding wagon loading length vary depending on the flexibility of the load.

Definition

Rigid long goods are transported as exceptional consignments when they are loaded on special wagons with pivoted bolster or rotary sliding bolsterand, if necessary, also with barrier/intermediate wagons.
