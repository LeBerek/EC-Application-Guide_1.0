In some networks or just on selected lines or sidings the maximum load may be lower. Always inspect the permissible maximum load on the entire planned route. Poland is one of the countries where just under 20% of paths have the maximum load capacity of 20.0 tonnes, and where an additional load of 0.5 t per wheelset in class C requires an exceptional consignment authorisation.
